This is an animation replacer especially intended for [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/12440]Immersive HUD[/url] and hidden crosshair.

[heading]Features[/heading]
[list]
[*] Replace first person crossbow animations to line up with the target.
[*] No plugin, safe to remove at any time.
[/list]

[heading]Installation[/heading]
Use your mod manager of choice, [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/skyrimspecialedition/mods/6194]Mod Organizer 2[/url] is recommended.
&#8203;
